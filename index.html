About Me

I’m Ajay Addada, a Computer Science graduate student at NJIT, focusing on AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Data Analytics.

I enjoy building end-to-end solutions: from data collection and cleaning, to training models and exposing them through simple, usable interfaces. Recently I’ve worked on RAG-based chatbots, YOLOv8 object detection pipelines, and a deepfake detection system that classifies media as real or fake.

My goal is to use AI to solve practical problems—especially in automation, security, and decision support—while keeping things explainable and easy to maintain for teams who use them.
